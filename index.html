Know Your Teacher
Shraddha Ankush
Founder · Certified Bellydance Instructor · Banjara Format
I created Stritva with one intention : to help women reconnect with their bodies, their intuition, and their inner fire through belly dance.

Trained under Meher Malik, I believe dance is more than technique : it's a journey of confidence, healing, and self-expression.

At Stritva, I welcome beginners and returning dancers into a warm, judgment-free space where you can learn, grow, and feel truly supported.

If you're looking for a space that feels safe, empowering, creative, & deeply feminine : welcome home to Stritva. 🌙
